"Hello Sharing world!"

CREATIVE COMMONS GLOBAL SUMMIT 2015

A joyful celebration beleiving in the value of openness and sharing, creating a positive change with people from over 80 countries is taking place in october, in korea.

2015.10.15(THU) ~ 16(FRI)

@National Museum of korea, Art Center Nabi and more



Main Program

Lecture

Listen to speaker from various countries about the messages of sharing and opening. There will aslo be various tenor of the music industries whom will share their love for music

CC Exhibition

Bikers all around the world will be performing to celebrate this unique event. Professional bikers will held activities around every concert stage. Local bikers will be awarded and more...

Forum

Have the time to share your thoughts and opinions with expert for each topic. People all around the world will discuss about rock music and the impact of rock culture in their society.

Workshop

Many workshops will be held all around the world for this special event to share and promote rock culture. Non-rock fans will have the unique opportunity connect with rock culture.

CC Ignite

Finally here comes what every one is waiting for. The ultimate Rock concert all around the world at the same time. Each concert will begin with a tribute to Chester Bennington performed by local band
